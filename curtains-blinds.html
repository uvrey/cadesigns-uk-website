Carol Anne Designs manufacture, supply and fit quality and durable designer ranges of curtains, blinds and shutters. We supply and fit different products and cost levels for both our professional landlords and our high-end bespoke clients.

Our clients can rely upon our expertise and knowledge to deliver and install products that meet their specific needs and requirements – be it specific roller blinds that fit in between outer and secondary glazing sash windows; roller blinds for privacy, sunscreen shading or blackout; roman blinds in fabrics that match the installed curtains; or modern wave curtain designs with durable flush track systems – both manual and electronically operated.

In line with new legislation aiming to avoid strangulation and entanglement, all Carol Anne Designs products are wholly compliant with the Child Safety Requirements of BS EN13120. We endeavour to offer a stylish and functional range of Child Safety accessories on all our products, and our primary Professional Landlords roller blind is manufactured to our own unique design and specification to achieve an aesthetically pleasing, durable and high-quality product.
