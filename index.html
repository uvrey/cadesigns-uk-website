About

Carol Anne Designs is a 30-year interior design studio based in West London.

The company focuses on delivering product knowledge, expertise and service at different cost and product range levels that meet the specific needs and requirements of both professional estate landlords and bespoke clients in the supply and installation of:-
Curtains, Blinds and Shutters. Painting, Decorating and Wallpapers. Carpets, Stair Runners and Rugs. Furniture, Soft Furnishings and Fabrics.
Carol Hainsworth is the founder and primary designer of the Studio and, along with her husband Peter and their highly experienced team, has built up an enviable reputation amongst their customers for service, delivery and quality.
The companies Bespoke Commissions includes spectacular houses, apartments, hotels and H.Q. reception foyers found in prime central London, Richmond, Wimbledon and the Cotswolds, commissioned by private clients, property developers, estate landlords and corporate H.Q’s. Carol works hand in hand with her husband Peter, who completes the Construction Project management and designs the Architectural Design elements of a client’s refurbishments program through the companies in house construction company CAD Property Refurbishments Ltd.
The company is an accredited contractor under the Alcumus SafeContractor Health & Safety scheme. The company has £5 million level of cover for Public Liability Insurance.
