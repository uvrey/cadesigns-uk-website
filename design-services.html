DESIGN SERVICES

CAD BESPOKE provides to clients an interior design practice with over 30 years of knowledge and experience working with high-end clients, primarily designing luxury residential properties, prime London hotels, Corporate HQ foyers, developers show-houses and communal block entrance foyers.

Each project, large or small, draws upon Carol’s interior design skills, knowledge and experience gained in Cape Town, Geneva and London to understand each client’s needs and individual style, subsequently executing and delivering a unique luxurious interior. Carol’s bespoke and individual one-to-one approach ensures each project is handled with meticulous service, care and attention to detail – consistently surpassing expectations.

CAD Bespoke offers a complete end to end interior design service encompassing and managing any and all processes from the initial idea / concept, design and planning phases, sourcing and designing construction materials and internal fittings and bespoke joinery, construction works, supplying of high-end furniture and soft furnishings, which we source from all over the world, eventually adding the final touches to our projects to create a finished, warm and comfortable environment suited to our client’s needs.

Our furniture products, lighting, chandeliers, pendants, fabrics, wallpapers and carpets are sourced from around the world, including handmade bespoke items. Our studio showroom has sample books of fabrics, wallpapers and carpets from our UK and worldwide suppliers that will meet most client’s needs. Our extensive range of Blinds & Curtains includes both manual and electronic operation which can be fitted into most homes and apartments.

Our portfolio of work includes various projects undertaken in Prime & Central London and large houses in Richmond, Wimbledon Village and the Cotswolds.
