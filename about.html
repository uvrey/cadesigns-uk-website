EXCELLENCE



Carol Anne Designs is a 30-year interior design studio based in West London, meeting the needs of professional estate landlords and bespoke clients.

Carol Hainsworth is the founder and primary designer of the Studio and, along with her husband Peter and their highly experienced team, has built up an enviable reputation amongst their customers for service, delivery and quality.

The company's Bespoke Commissions include spectacular houses, apartments, hotels and H.Q. reception foyers in prime central London, Richmond, Wimbledon and the Cotswolds, commissioned by private clients, property developers, estate landlords and corporates.


The companies Bespoke Commissions includes spectacular houses, apartments, hotels and H.Q. reception foyers found in prime central London, Richmond, Wimbledon and the Cotswolds, commissioned by private clients, property developers, estate landlords and corporate H.Q’s.

Carol works hand in hand with her husband Peter, who completes the Construction Project management and designs the Architectural Design elements of a client’s refurbishments program through the companies in house construction company CAD Property Refurbishments Ltd.

The company is an accredited contractor under the Alcumus SafeContractor Health & Safety scheme. The company has £5 million level of cover for Public Liability Insurance.
