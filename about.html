Carol Anne Designs is an interior design studio based in West London. For over 30 years, our Studio has created beautiful spaces for bespoke clients and rental investments.

Carol Hainsworth is the founder and primary designer. Together with her team and husband, Peter, she brings a wealth of experience in furnishings, decor and fabrics.

The Studio provides a complete interior design service. Carol and her team carry out each stage of the design process, from ideation to project implementation. We take care to use only exceptional materials, such as high-end furniture and soft furnishings, to manifest each client’s vision for their home or rental investment space.

Our portfolio of work includes various projects undertaken in Prime & Central London, as well as large houses in Richmond, Wimbledon Village and the Cotswolds.
