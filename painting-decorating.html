Carol and our team are skilled decorators, whose painting and decorating services range from identifying and ordering fabrics for soft furnishings to creating unique wall coverings. We are highly experienced in planning and carrying out seamless painting and decorating work, which we arrange according to our clients' needs.

Over the last 30 years, we have established strong partnerships with a community of designers and makers, who continue to provide us with extraordinary pieces to incorporate in our projects. These include light fittings, joinery, and many other eye-catching smaller details to complement larger furnishings.

Our studio showroom in London highlights the wide range of fabrics, wallpapers, light fittings, and handmade pieces we bring in to our projects.



Our interior services include both high-quality interior painting and paperhanging. Be it a rental investment property, a Mayfair ambassadorial house, a Barnes home or a Cotswolds country house our tradesmen take great pride in their work, are highly skilled, and deliver an expert finish that will continue to look brilliant for many years. Our work is protected under the Safe Contractor umbrella, and we carry £5 millions of public liability insurance cover as required by our Professional Trade Landlord clients. Our team will provide help, advice and recommendations to achieve your creative vision, meet your budget and timescales; all without compromising on quality.
